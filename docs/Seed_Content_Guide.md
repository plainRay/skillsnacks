# SkillSnacks Seed Content Guide

Codex should generate a small starter content library so the application is usable immediately after setup.

## Minimum Seed Content

### Categories
At least 4 categories:
- science
- history
- technology
- culture

### Lessons
At least 8 lessons total.
Recommended minimum:
- 2 science lessons
- 2 history lessons
- 2 technology lessons
- 2 culture lessons

### Topics
At least 2 topics:
- cosmic_origins
- abby_medical_terminology

The medical topic can be included as a demo learning track even if lesson count is small.

---

## Lesson Writing Pattern

Each lesson should include:
- a concise title
- a short reading time
- a why section
- 3 to 5 key ideas
- a curiosity hook

Lessons should feel like micro-learning, not textbook chapters.

---

## Example Lesson Titles

### Science
- The Big Bang
- Why Do Stars Twinkle?

### History
- Why Did the Roman Empire Fall?
- The Printing Press and Its Impact

### Technology
- What Is Cloud Computing?
- How Does GPS Work?

### Culture
- Why Do Humans Tell Stories?
- The Origins of Jazz

### Medical
- Introduction to Medical Terms
- Common Medical Prefixes

---

## Seed Content Goal

The project should feel like a real working prototype, not an empty scaffold.
